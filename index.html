About me

I am currently a senior at Montclair State University in pursuit for my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I will be expecting to graduate in the spring of May 2018. As a senior, I had taken several classes for my major in order to prepare for my future in programming as well as self-teaching myself several programming languages throughout my college experience. My goal is to create website contents that will satisfy viewers and customers with the information provided in a clean and beautiful fashion. My goal is to make a difference in this world, to allow people, like myself, to have an opinion strong enough to be heard and I hope that the websites I will be able to work on can catch the attention of the world and allow me to show them what I got.

Most recently, I finished the app, MSUCampusRec, for my Web Development job at Montclair State Campus Recreation. The task was to redesign the app from its previous design and create more features to better the productivity of the Campus Recreation for the Montclair State University community. I have also been working on the website montclair.edu/campus-recreation, creating pages and events for the gym using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My current project is working on transitioning the website into wordpress for Campus Recreation's 10th anniversity.
